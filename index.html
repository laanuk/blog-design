Kanscience

"Never do anything against your conscience even if the state demands it." - Albert Einstein

Though he is deemed the father of quantum physics, explorer of black holes, and a pioneer of what lay beyond, Einstein found the most balancing aspect of human nature in one of the most immediate places — inward!

Whether I am stuck between the waves of conflicting emotions or lost in the fabric of time, it is my conscience that allows me to return. And perhaps it is for the same reason why I have always wanted to find my voice and convey my thoughts to the world. Thus, I bring you Kanscience — a communication portal to share the deepest and most vibrant part of myself. Hopefully, it will help you make a similar connection within your inner self.
